Everything About Ashley Marti From Below Deck Sailing Yacht

August 2024 · 3 minute read

The third season of the yacht drama “Below Deck Sailing Yacht” has both new and returning cast members. Ashley Marti will portray the third stew in Parsifal III, which will premiere on Bravo TV on February 21, 2022, at 8:00 p.m. EST.

The program has just released the preview for the upcoming season, which has grabbed the interest of fans. The short video depicted great drama and turbulent seas. Can the deckhands be able to accomplish this voyage effectively and without incident?

What is Ashley Marti’s background in the third season of Below Deck Sailing Yacht?

Ashley Marti was born in New York before moving to southern Florida. She left New York City due to a strong desire to live near the ocean, which prompted her to relocate.

Ashley chose to begin her professional career in the boating industry during the outbreak, and her passion for the water ensured that she never looked back.

Working on the East Coast of the United States and in the Bahamas provided Ashley Marti with sailing experience. She is currently preparing to embark on the Parsifal III and eagerly anticipates more thrilling marine adventures.

With the photo, Ashley Marti announced her intention to join the deck on Instagram.

“Let’s get started! #BelowDeckSailing premieres February 21 on @BravoTV! Bravo, @BelowDeckBravo! The trailer is accessible on the BravoTV website.”

Marti has also danced at nightclubs. She was a part of the dance group Daredevils, which has performed at live performances by DJs and musicians including Martin Garixx, Dillon Francis, and Lil Tunichi.

Kitty Obrien and Miranda Minick are Ashley Marti’s closest friends. The women can gather and have fun together regularly, enjoying a respite from their busy lives on the boat.

Relationship between Ashley and her shipmate Kelsie Goglia

The flight attendant appears to be close friends with co-star Kelsie Goglia. These shipmates saw waterfalls, beaches, and yachts while having a fantastic time together.

In season 3, numerous newcomers will join Ashley on the deck, including chef Marcos Spaziani, second stew Gabriela Barragan, Tom Pearson, and Kelsie Goglia.
