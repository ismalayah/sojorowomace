Career

She began her career in the 80s by making small appearances in various serials like Good Morning, Who’s the boss?, The Wonder Years, Doggie Howser and Falcon Crest. She reprised her role for the film’s sequels in 2002 and 2003. In 2001 she starred in another movie with Jet Li called The One. In 2003 she starred in a series called Karen Sisco and in 2005 she starred in a science fiction series called Threshold.
