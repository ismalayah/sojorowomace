July 2024 · 1 minute read

Wayne Duvall is an American actor known for appearing as Homer Stokes in O Brother, Where Art Thou, as well as recurring roles in the television series Billions, The District, The Leftovers, BrainDead, The Righteous Gemstones, and others.

Duvall was born in Silver Spring, Maryland, and is a cousin of the actor Robert Duvall. Duvall has also appeared in films like Apollo 13, Leatherheads, Duplicity, Lincoln, American Animals, Richard Jewell, The Hunt, and The Trial of the Chicago 7, as well as in Broadway theatre productions, most notably the original New York run of 1984.
